faq

why compromise when every detail can be just right?

at seetrue, quality and taste aren't just promises—they're our standard. from the precision in craftsmanship to the care in how it reaches you, everything is designed to feel effortless and natural.

but in the end, the choice is always yours.

it started with a gift. aria, one of the three brothers, created a pair of glasses for their uncle ali. he wore them everywhere because they elevated his outfit and made him stand out—something uncle ali truly loves.

as more family and friends noticed, the three brothers knew they had created something special. today, every pair is named after someone close to the brothers, keeping those connections and meanings alive in every design.

of course—uv protection is standard. but why settle for just protection?

with seetrue glasses, others see through to your eyes, turning them into more than just eyewear—they're an accessory for every moment, indoors or out.

absolutely. seetrue glasses are designed to transition effortlessly between any setting, making them the perfect accessory for every moment—day or night.

we choose materials the way a tailor chooses fabric — with intention.

seetrue frames are crafted from high-quality acetate, known for its depth of colour, lightness on the face, and long-lasting resilience. each sheet is hand-selected and cut with precision so that the pattern, weight, and finish all align exactly as designed.

some pieces in our limited collections incorporate hand-polished titanium temples for added durability and a refined finish that age beautifully over time.

every detail around the glasses matters as much as the glasses themselves.

each seetrue pair arrives in a rigid case, lined in soft suede, shaped to hold the frame without pressure. inside you'll find a cleaning cloth, a small care card, and a personal note — because we believe the unboxing should feel like the beginning of something.

limited edition pieces include additional packaging as part of the collection experience.
